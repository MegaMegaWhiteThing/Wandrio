Wandrio Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2.3.2025

Last Updated: 6.5.2025

Wandrio ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our services, including the Wandrio app, website, and related features.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and password.

Profile Information: You may choose to add details such as settings and preferences.

Payment Information: If you purchase premium features or in-game items, we process payment details via third-party payment processors.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Location Data: We collect GPS data to track tile visits and points of interest (POIs).

Device Information: We gather device type, operating system, and app version for optimization and security.

Usage Data: Includes interactions with tiles, map layers, and activity tracking.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services (Strava & Garmin)

If you choose to link your Strava or Garmin account, we collect:

Activity Data: Distance, routes, timestamps, and elevation.

Visited Locations: GPS coordinates from your recorded activities to visualize tiles and POIs.

You can manage or revoke this connection in your Wandrio account settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Wandrio platform.
  • Process and display activity data from Strava and Garmin to enhance tile and POI visualizations.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, offers, or support.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your data. However, we may share your information in the following cases:

  • With Service Providers: Third-party partners assist with hosting, analytics, and payments.
  • With Strava & Garmin: Only if you choose to link your accounts, we may send and receive relevant activity data.
  • For Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect our rights and users.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including encryption and access controls. However, no method of transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices & Rights

Access & Correction: You can update or delete your profile information anytime.

Location Tracking: You can disable location tracking in your device settings, but it may limit key features.

Strava & Garmin Integration: You can disconnect your accounts at any time in settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

Wandrio is not intended for users under 13. If we learn that a child’s data has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Significant changes will be communicated to users.
