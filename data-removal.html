Delete Your Wandrio Data

This applies to the Wandrio mobile app.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, log in and go to your profile page. Tap the Delete Account button and confirm. All data will be removed within 24 hours.

If You Cannot Log In

Email support@wandrioapp.com from your account email and request deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Location history
  • Saved places
  • User profile and preferences

Data Retention

All user data is deleted immediately upon request. However, system logs and backups may be retained for up to 30 days before being permanently purged.
